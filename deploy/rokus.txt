; Enter the list of all of your Roku devices on your network, along with their username and password
; Should make it easier to update the channel on all of your devices
; Remember: Every time you update the app on your Roku, it will start the channel immediately upon success.
; So if you are watching something on one of your Rokus and you run this script, it will be interrupted.
; Format is:
;<RokuIP><space>rokudev:<RokuPassword>
;ex:
;192.168.1.52 rokudev:rokupass
